Tokyo

Days 1-2:

Upon arrival at Tokyo Narida Airport, I was picked up in a shuttle bus and made my way into the city centre. Our hotel was called Hotel Niwa in the Chiyoda District. I dropped off my bags and decided to go for a walk to the nearest park to get my first close-up view of the newly bloomed cherry blossom trees. I found Koishikawa Korakuen Garden and managed to take some beautiful pictures. The weather was very sunny and I was comfortable in my jeans and t-shirt.

On my way back fromt he park, I took a small detour around the streets to soak in Tokyo for the first time ever!

In the evening, I met my fellow tour members and we had our first meal together. We introduced ourselves and I quickly realised I was the youngest by around 40 years! Also, I was very sad that the hotel served European food on the first day!
